Beef Tehari

Beef Tehari. Tehari is an Indian menu made of rice and small pieces of beef. Although it looks like biryani, the difference is made by the lesser quantity of beef.

BBQ Chicken

Barbecue chicken consists of chicken parts or entire chickens that are barbecued, grilled or smoked. There are many global and regional preparation techniques and cooking styles. Barbecue chicken is often seasoned or coated in a spice rub, barbecue sauce, or both

Nihari

Nihari is a stew from the desi subcontinent consisting of slow-cooked meat, mainly shank meat of beef or lamb and mutton, goat meat and chicken, along with bone marrow. It is flavored with long pepper, a relative of black pepper

Mutton Chaap

A mutton gravy dish cooked in cardamom, mace and cinnamon that The lamb chops are slow cooked in this thick and luscious gravy which helps the mutton pieces to become more tender and juicy. The flavour of the curry will also help to seep into the Lamb chops and create a delicious side dish.

Keema Parata

Keema Paratha is a flatbread where the spicy and flavourful minced mutton mixture is stuffed into a whole wheat flour dough and then rolled to make a delicious paratha..
